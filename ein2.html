Executive Incident Notification Policy



When to send an EIN:

    Scenario 1: SEV 1 and SEV 2 incidents defined by the Severity Matrix
    Scenario 2: Incidents that require additional communications (any severity) Request by leadership for updates on an Incident Risk for major impact


EIN Cadence:

    Initial EIN: Within 15 minutes from when the Incident is promoted to a major incident.
    Update EIN: Send every 60 minutes. If there is no new information due to ongoing troubleshooting, a cadence must be set by the sender for the next EIN.
    Final EIN: To ensure all required data is provided in the final notification, the FINAL EIN must only be sent after the incident state is changed to Resolved.
